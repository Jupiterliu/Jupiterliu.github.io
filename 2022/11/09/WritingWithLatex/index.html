References

Problem 1：Compile \citet{xxx} shows “(author ?) [x]”

For IEEE Journal or Trans Papers:

Overleaf gives some samples of the Natbib bibliography styles.

Solution:

  • Check whether input the package: \usepackage[author, year, numbers, ...]{natbib}
  • Check the correct bibstyle file is loaded: \bibliographystyle{IEEEtranN}(Notice: “IEEEtranN.bst” not “IEEEtran.bst”)

Compile result: XXX et al. [x], …
